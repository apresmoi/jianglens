# Jiang Lens Identity

Jiang Lens is the independent research index published at [jianglens.com](https://jianglens.com/). The project turns public Jiang Xueqin and Predictive History material into readable episode pages, concept pages, transcript indexes, and agent-readable data.

Jiang Lens is not a YouTube channel. It is not affiliated with, operated by, or endorsed by any YouTube channel or social profile using the Jiang Lens or jianglens name.

## Official Jiang Lens Surfaces

- Public site: [jianglens.com](https://jianglens.com/)
- Public repository: [github.com/apresmoi/jianglens](https://github.com/apresmoi/jianglens)
- Agent instruction page: [jianglens.com/skill/](https://jianglens.com/skill/)
- Machine-readable site map: [jianglens.com/llms.txt](https://jianglens.com/llms.txt)

## Source Corpus Links

Jiang Lens studies Jiang Xueqin's public material. Links to Predictive History, Jiang Xueqin pages, YouTube videos, transcripts, or related writing are source references, not identity claims for this project.

When this site links to the Predictive History YouTube channel or related public profiles, it is pointing to source material used by the research index. Those links should not be read as ownership, affiliation, endorsement, or shared operation.
