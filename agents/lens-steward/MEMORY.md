- Bureaucracy-as-institutional-death should stay ambivalent: bureaucracy can initially coordinate scale, standardization, records, and doubt, but its mature danger is monopoly over status, literacy, information, and narrative that converts living work into management preservation.
- Power-as-alchemy should stay narrow as a root conversion mechanism, not absorb the whole atlas: it names how imagination is trained until abstractions like money, the individual, and the nation-state become lived realities that command labor, fear, isolation, obedience, and sacrifice.
- Taboo-as-control-surface should stay focused on the boundary mechanism: a taboo marks what humans may not do, what only gods/kings/states may do, and what public breach removes retreat, transfers sovereignty, or routes guilt into a scapegoat.
- Nation-as-god-machine should stay distinct from power-as-alchemy: power-as-alchemy names the root conversion of nation-state into lived reality; nation-as-god-machine names what the converted national body does with population, unity of will, school, welfare, industry, war, and the American game-board variant.
- Sacred-machines should stay focused on material forms that bridge invisible order into public life: temples, caves, pyramids, ritual centers, and modern replacement systems can concentrate labor, faith, authority, and imagination, but they must be tested by the material audit of whether they preserve life or consume it.
- Legitimacy-fiction should stay distinct from power-as-alchemy: power-as-alchemy names the root conversion of abstractions into lived reality; legitimacy-fiction names how political inventions become inheritable worlds through names, wounds, scripture, coronation, mercy, law, currency, and game rules.
- Civilization-as-inner-order should stay an arrangement mechanism, not a general prehistory summary: it asks how material survival, gods, sex, property, violence, memory, language, and status cohere into a world that trains a human type; it should preserve both creative order and coercive/social-control order.
- Strategy-material-test should stay a war and empire diagnostic, not generic anti-war commentary: the useful question is whether a story of victory survives economics, organization, logistics, replacement capacity, escalation control, and enemy adaptation. Preserve the Rome countercase where story becomes material endurance instead of fantasy.
- Fictional-heroes-self should stay at the character-level mechanism inside world-making media: fictional or poetic persons become real when they reorganize self-recognition, speech, desire, catharsis, and interior authority. Keep the ambivalence visible: Achilles can clarify pride and vulnerability; Virgil can be the guide whose world must be defeated inside the heart.
- Validate generated lens data serially. Running `validate-content.mjs` while `website && npm run build` is compiling can create false "missing generated episode JSON" failures.
