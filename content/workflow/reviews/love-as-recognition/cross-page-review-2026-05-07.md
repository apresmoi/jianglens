## Review Question

Should **Love As Recognition** move from proposal seed into a public lens page now, become an atlas relation, or remain a reviewed boundary proposal?

The seed is real and source-backed. Jiang repeatedly treats love as more than affection: secret signs let a person recognize another across danger; Priam's grief makes Achilles human again; Virgilian piety can turn love into disease, mission, or vengeance; Dante makes love relational rather than abstract; psychohistory says blocked love reverses into hatred; Secret History ties love to free will and non-coercive trust.

The promotion question is not whether the mechanism exists. It is whether a new public page would clarify the atlas more than the existing public surfaces already do.

## Decision

Hold **Love As Recognition** as a reviewed proposal seed. Do not promote it to a public concept page or atlas relation yet.

The current public lens already gives the active layers stronger homes:

- `Human Heart As Civilizational Measure` owns the broad civilizational test: love, guilt, pride, forgiveness, reason's limit, and blocked love returning as hate.
- `Free Will As Cosmic Burden` owns love as trust, divine nonintervention, agency, chosen slavery, and institutional anti-love.
- `Education As A Soul Game` owns love as formation pressure when homes and schools turn affection into performance, anxiety, grades, or obedience.
- `How Poetry Creates Civilization`, `How Fictional Heroes Become Part Of The Self`, and `The Guide Who Becomes A Trap` own the literary media layers: Homeric recognition, Virgil's false love, Dido's memory, Beatrice, and guide language.
- `Civilization As Inner Order` owns Priam/Achilles when the question is what kind of human being a civilization trains.
- `Eschatology` owns love when it becomes salvation, hell, divine light, non-contractual rescue, and free will inside a cosmic script.

A new public page would be justified only if it owned the relational mechanism itself: how love recognizes and enlarges a concrete person, while anti-love turns the beloved into proof, property, mission, obedience, credential, or revenge. The proposal has that boundary, but the public map does not yet need another entry point to prevent confusion. At present, promotion would risk duplicating strong pages and encouraging over-linking every "love" phrase to one attractive abstraction.

## Promotion Conditions

Promote only after at least one of these becomes true:

1. A later source gives love-as-recognition as an explicit reusable mechanism rather than a dispersed Great Books and Secret History pattern.
2. Existing pages begin accumulating duplicate "what love is" sections that would be clearer as one relation page.
3. Episode links need durable anchors that existing heart, free-will, education, poetry, fictional-heroes, guide-trap, civilization, or eschatology anchors cannot honestly carry.
4. A public page can stay concrete and relational: secret language, enemy recognition, anti-love as instrumentality, relation as divine light, and forgiveness/growth without becoming a "Jiang on love" catalog.
5. The page can preserve Jiang's severity: love may humiliate power, test the returned husband, wound the father, refuse vengeance for the dead, and deny the comfort of managing the beloved.

## Routing Diagnostic

Use **love-as-recognition** as a proposal boundary for now:

- Route to `Human Heart` when the question is whether social order honors love, guilt, forgiveness, reason's limits, or blocked-love reversal.
- Route to `Free Will` when love requires trust, agency, nonintervention, non-contractual salvation, or resistance to chosen slavery.
- Route to `Education` when love is converted into grades, credentials, anxious parenting, trust-transfer, or formation.
- Route to `Poetry` or `Fictional Heroes` when the scene works through media, character, memory, language, or self-recognition.
- Route to `Guide-Trap` when a mediator translates love into duty, debt, obedience, or false explanation.
- Route to `Civilization` when love changes the image of the human being a civilization trains.
- Route to `Eschatology` when love functions as salvation, hell, cosmic script, or divine light.

The future public page, if needed, should own only the relation test: does this bond make the other more visible, more alive, more free, and more capable of growth, or does it turn the other into an instrument of mission, property, proof, revenge, obedience, credential, or control?

## Review Outcome

Approved as a boundary-preserving review. Do not promote **Love As Recognition** now. The review advances the lens map by preventing a broad but attractive seed from blurring several mature public concepts while preserving the exact conditions under which promotion would later clarify the atlas.
