# Cult Of The Self Isolated Freedom - Cross-Page Review

Date: 2026-05-07
Review type: promotion boundary / cross-page review
Decision: hold as reviewed proposal seed

## Decision

Do not promote **Cult Of The Self As Isolated Freedom** to a public concept page or atlas relation yet.

The seed names a real Jiang mechanism: isolation is sold as freedom, selfhood becomes an inward proof or display project, shared action and recognition weaken, and power benefits because separated people are easier to manage. That mechanism is strong enough to preserve as a proposal seed, but the current public map already gives each active layer a clearer home. A standalone page now would repeat neighboring pages more than it would clarify the atlas.

This review therefore closes the current promotion question conservatively:

- no public lens page;
- no atlas relation;
- no durable lens points;
- no episode-to-lens links.

Use the seed as routing discipline until later sources create repeated confusion that only a public isolation-to-control page can solve.

## Source Pressure

The central source remains **2025-06-04**, [Kill The Cult Of The Self](/episodes/predictive-history-cylkqpsffry/). Jiang makes the modern individual a religious problem before he makes it psychological, artistic, political, or technological: monotheism creates the direct God-relation and removes the person from community (`video:predictive-history-cylkqpsffry@transcript:v1#seg-0003`), Protestantism intensifies that into a crisis of faith (`video:predictive-history-cylkqpsffry@transcript:v1#seg-0005`), and modernism is later named as a cult of self-improvement and self-empowerment (`video:predictive-history-cylkqpsffry@transcript:v1#seg-0017`).

The proposal's strongest political formulation comes through the duplicated/re-uploaded Civilization #57 read: if the self is treated as the source of everything, collective action becomes impossible; isolated freedom becomes nothingness and slavery; real freedom requires recognition from other free people (`video:predictive-history-ra2v9hkahz8@transcript:v1#seg-0051`, `video:predictive-history-ra2v9hkahz8@transcript:v1#seg-0052`, `video:predictive-history-ra2v9hkahz8@transcript:v1#seg-0053`).

The source pressure is real, but it is still concentrated. The current public map should not convert one large lecture chain and its duplicate into a new public family before the surrounding pages have a demonstrated navigation problem.

## Cross-Page Routing

### Power As Alchemy

[Power As Alchemy](/lens/power-as-alchemy/) already owns the upstream conversion of the individual into a lived abstraction. It also owns the privatization of distress: misery moves inside the isolated person, collective action becomes difficult, and expert authority enters the vacuum. The cult-of-self seed should point here when the mechanism is individual responsibility, therapy, diagnosis, self-management, or private defect replacing shared social contest.

Boundary: cult-of-self would only need its own public surface if the core mechanism is not just privatized distress, but freedom itself being redefined as an inward project that disables relation and action.

### Attention Capture As Capital Extraction

[Attention Capture As Capital Extraction](/lens/attention-capture-as-capital-extraction/) owns screens, social media, AI, anxiety, debt, work discipline, and captured focus when they extract attention as energy or organize consciousness as wealth. The cult-of-self seed should point here when the technological surface captures attention, stores consciousness, returns anxiety to work, or makes leisure another wall.

Boundary: cult-of-self would only own social media when the source explicitly says the platform democratizes self-display, self-indulgence, self-measurement, self-obsession, or self-worship as a freedom practice.

### Free Will As Cosmic Burden

[Free Will As Cosmic Burden](/lens/free-will-as-cosmic-burden/) owns chosen slavery, chosen hell, anti-love as denial of agency, and the refusal to let institutions carry the soul's responsibility. It should receive material where Jiang asks what the soul chooses, wants, delegates, or calls freedom inside a false world.

Boundary: cult-of-self would own the social technology that sells isolated inwardness as freedom before that freedom becomes a free-will burden. It should not absorb chosen slavery or chosen hell unless the source foregrounds the self-cult machinery.

### Human Heart As Civilizational Measure

The cult-of-self seed should point here when the main question is whether an order honors or represses the heart.

Boundary: cult-of-self would own anti-relation only when the mechanism is isolation made attractive as freedom and then converted into controllability. It should not become a general page for love, recognition, depression, or alienation.

### How Poetry Creates Civilization

[How Poetry Creates Civilization](/lens/how-poetry-creates-civilization/) already owns the modernism/Joyce negative case from Civilization #57. Literature reverses poetry's democratic power when shared truth becomes elite allusion, private consciousness, artist-as-god, and self-salvation. That public mutation is enough for the literary layer.

Boundary: cult-of-self should not duplicate the Joyce section. It should only touch modernism or art when the source uses art to spread liberal individualism, self-worship, or incapacity for collective action beyond the literary access problem.

### Love As Recognition

`content/workflow/proposals/love-as-recognition/proposal.md` and its cross-page review remain the better home for concrete relational recognition: love sees and enlarges a specific person through relation, remorse, growth, forgiveness, and mutual freedom. Cult-of-self is the negative control chain, not the positive love mechanism.

Boundary: if later material centers recognition as cure, route to love-as-recognition or human-heart unless the source is primarily diagnosing isolation as control.

### Mass Society As Political Constraint

[Mass Society As Political Constraint](/lens/mass-society-as-political-constraint/) owns scale pressure: feeding, organizing, occupying, spiritually stabilizing, and managing millions or billions. It should receive material where individual isolation is downstream of machine-function, artificial elites, social control, or population-scale management.

Boundary: cult-of-self would only need public promotion if the self-cult itself becomes a scale technology distinct from attention capture, school, bureaucracy, nation, or mass-society control.

## Reader / World-Model Judge

The seed is useful because it names a recurring danger in Jiang's modernity model: freedom can be made inward, private, expressive, therapeutic, or self-salvational until the person has fewer relations through which to act. The formulation preserves Jiang's pressure better than generic "individualism is bad" language.

The weakness is public duplication. A reader who lands on a standalone cult-of-self page today would likely be sent immediately to power-as-alchemy for the individual, attention capture for social media, poetry for Joyce, free will for chosen false freedom, human heart for recognition, and mass society for machine-function. That means the page would be a routing hub more than a new lens.

No public promotion is warranted until additional dated sources make the isolation-to-control mechanism recur independently enough that readers need a single entry point.
