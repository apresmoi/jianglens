## Decision

Hold **Population As Weapon** as a reviewed proposal seed. Do not promote it to a public concept page or atlas relation yet.

The proposal names a real Jiang reversal: the population that the nation-state grows, educates, heals, mobilizes, and sacralizes as strength can later become the surface through which war attacks the state. The April 28, 2026 source makes that reversal explicit when twenty-first-century war shifts from killing soldiers or destroying factories toward using discord, dissent, economic sabotage, infrastructure pressure, and civilian anger so the population turns against its own government.

That relation is important, but the current public map already carries the active mechanisms more clearly:

- **Nation As God-Machine** owns the upstream national machinery and the direct target reversal: population becomes capacity, then target.
- **Mass Society As Political Constraint** owns the scale problem: billions must be fed, organized, occupied, soothed, surveilled, and managed.
- **Strategy Material Test** owns the material attack surface: infrastructure, energy, food, logistics, replacement curves, and cost.
- **Eschatology As Script** owns the counter-war technology when suffering is reinterpreted as divine plan, martyrdom, and sacred endurance.
- **Attention Capture** owns attention and consciousness extraction when morale, focus, consumer desire, or narrative capture is the operative channel.

Promoting the seed now would mostly restate those pages. The better use is boundary discipline: route future source moments by the mechanism doing the work, and keep this seed available only when a later public surface needs the cross-page reversal itself.

## Promotion Test

Promotion would be justified only if a future mutation can answer all of these without becoming a population-war topic page:

1. Where does the source show population first becoming state or national capacity?
2. Where does the source show an opponent targeting the population-state relation rather than only killing people or destroying infrastructure?
3. What channel turns population into weapon: necessity, anger, ethnic trust, information, attention, economic pressure, or morale?
4. Which existing public page owns that channel better?
5. Does the source add something the existing Nation, Mass Society, Strategy, Eschatology, or Attention pages cannot carry?
6. Would a public page reduce duplication, or would it create a generic war/demography hub?

## Routing Rule

Use **Population As Weapon** only as a boundary seed unless the cross-page reversal itself becomes the missing public surface:

> Population becomes a weapon when a state-built or mass-managed population is pressured through necessities, infrastructure, economy, trust, information, or anger until the people become the means by which the state is attacked.

Route the same source pressure by active mechanism:

- nation capacity or population target surface -> `nation-as-god-machine`
- scale management, feeding, occupation, surveillance, morale burden -> `mass-society-as-political-constraint`
- energy, fertilizer, logistics, infrastructure, replacement capacity -> `strategy-material-test`
- suffering as divine plan, martyrdom, committed minority endurance -> `eschatology`
- attention, consciousness, anxiety, consumer/social focus capture -> `attention-capture`

## Result

Keep **Population As Weapon** as a reviewed proposal seed, not a public page and not an atlas relation. The proposal advances the lens map by preserving a precise reversal and preventing neighboring pages from absorbing population-war material loosely. The next useful mutation is either another source recurrence outside the April 2026 lecture, or a future atlas relation only if multiple public pages begin needing the same population-reversal explanation.
