## Question

Should **Truth As Predictive Coherence** remain only a proposal seed, become a standalone public concept page, or be held as the compact atlas relation already present in `website/src/content/docs/lens.md`?

## Decision

Promote only as an atlas relation. Do not create a standalone public concept page yet.

The proposal names a real cross-cutting Jiang mechanism: prediction matters because a strong account should bind past, present, and future. But "prediction" changes meaning across Jiang's sources. It can mean poetic truth beyond time, historical model correction, a visible game intervention, an eschatological script, or a caveated proof test inside speculative Secret History. A standalone page would currently risk turning those neighboring mechanisms into one over-broad prophecy/epistemology page.

The existing atlas relation is the right public boundary. It gives readers a routing test without absorbing the neighboring pages:

- [How Poetry Creates Civilization](/lens/how-poetry-creates-civilization/) owns prophetic truth when poet, prophet, teacher, beauty, language, and timeless truth are primary.
- [Living School For Psychohistory](/lens/living-school-for-psychohistory/) owns model formation, correction, and the institutional work of training people who can build future-facing history.
- [Game Theory](/lens/game-theory/) owns prediction when visible models, incentives, players, and intervention in the board are primary.
- [Eschatology As Script](/lens/eschatology/) owns prophecy when a future story assigns roles and coordinates action.
- [Secret Society As Coordination Technology](/lens/secret-society-as-coordination-technology/) owns the proof caveat in the speculative coordination register: prediction may strengthen a theory without proving causation.
