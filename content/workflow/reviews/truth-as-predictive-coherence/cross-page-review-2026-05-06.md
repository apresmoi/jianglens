## Decision

Hold **Truth As Predictive Coherence** as a reviewed proposal seed. Do not promote it to a public lens page yet.

The seed is real: Jiang uses prediction as a truth test in more than one register. In the poetic register, truth can contain past, present, and future because the poet-prophet speaks from a timeless field. In the modeling register, a historical account approaches truth when it connects the past, explains the present, and predicts the future while remaining caveated against proof and causation.

The current public lens, however, already gives each active mechanism a better home. A new public page would clarify the atlas only if it owned the epistemic crossing itself: how Jiang moves from truth to prediction without flattening prophecy, model correction, game intervention, eschatological scripting, or speculative conspiracy proof.
