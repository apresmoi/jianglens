### Nation And Power

Nation owns infrastructure when the machinery makes the national body, American world board, dollar game, population system, welfare capacity, or war machine playable. When the active claim is that an abstraction has become real enough to command work, obedience, or belief, route to Power.

### Strategy

Strategy owns infrastructure when it becomes a material war gate: chokepoints, blockades, energy systems, civilian infrastructure, logistics, payment routes, access control, or naval attrition. The strategy page already includes sea lanes, Hormuz, Malacca, civilian-infrastructure attack, hybrid war against state capacity, and the reserve-currency shift from circulation to coercive access control.

Platform infrastructure should not absorb war infrastructure simply because the word infrastructure appears. Use Strategy when the question is whether the story of control survives ships, repair yards, food, energy, water, cyber pressure, supply lines, chokepoints, or civilian anger.

### Secret Society

Secret Society owns hidden coordination only when secrecy, trust, transgression, layers, private rooms, cross-silo movement, or script-mediated coordination does real work. Hidden infrastructure is not automatically secret-society evidence. The seed should not become a soft conspiracy page where any unseen pipe implies a hidden network.

Use Platform Infrastructure only for the visible-interface/deeper-infrastructure diagnostic. Use Secret Society when the infrastructure is secondary to trust, secrecy, layered access, or an informal network moving through public silos.
