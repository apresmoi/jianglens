## Decision

Hold the seed. Do not promote `Reason As Replacement Religion` to a public page yet.

The proposal identifies a real mechanism that none of the neighboring pages owns cleanly: modernity keeps religion's structure while changing its sacred grammar. It replaces faith, orthodoxy, and truth with reason, debate, and progress; then the French Revolution transfers religious loyalty, sacrifice, myth, scapegoat logic, and messianic action into nation and war.

That is a coherent public-page candidate, but the current source base still concentrates around the April 2025 reason/French Revolution chain. The concept should become public only if promotion can keep a narrow substitution sequence rather than becoming a general page about every secular institution that behaves religiously.
