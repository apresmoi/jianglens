## Question

Should **Creativity As Reception** move toward a public concept page or atlas relation, or remain a reviewed proposal seed for routing future work?

## Decision

Hold as a reviewed proposal seed. Do not promote to a public page or atlas relation yet.

The mechanism is real: in Jiang's model, a living idea, image, story, truth, or world can arrive before the visible process, and later craft works backward to make the received whole communicable, testable, teachable, or inhabitable. The proposal is valuable because it prevents a recurring source pressure from being routed only by surface topic. But a public surface now would mostly restate work already carried by stronger neighboring pages.

Promote later only if duplicated public prose or stronger source pressure requires one place to teach the received-whole/backward-craft mechanism itself.

## Reader / World-Model Review

### Education

[Education As A Soul Game](/lens/education-as-a-soul-game/) already owns the school machine: stakeholder incentives, separation, trust transfer, meritocratic sorting, status failure, rival formation, and research as exploration. The creativity proposal should not steal the education page's institutional diagnosis. It is useful only when school procedure is contrasted with the arrival of an idea and the later craft that makes that idea shareable.

The Secret History #9 source gives that narrow contrast cleanly: school teaches research, hypothesis, experiment, outline, thesis, draft, and revision, while Jiang says actual thought often runs the other way. That is a boundary pressure, not yet a new education surface.

### Poetry And Stories

[How Poetry Creates Civilization](/lens/how-poetry-creates-civilization/) owns language, memory, rhythm, soul gateway, poetic possession, and civilizational world-making. [How Stories Control Reality](/lens/how-stories-control-reality/) owns story as world-setting, commandless coordination, living memory, narrative feedback, and guide-danger. The creativity seed touches these pages only when the source emphasizes origin and craft: a world arrives, then poetry, scripture, argument, teaching, or narrative form makes it transmissible.

The Bible and Dante examples are therefore supporting evidence, not promotion proof. Literary Genesis belongs mainly to story-control and human-heart because living scripture trains argument, forgiveness, and judgment. Dante belongs mainly to poetry and guide-trap because memorized language enters the psyche and an inherited cosmology can be remade. Creativity-as-reception preserves one cross-cutting question: where did the living whole come from, and how was it made shareable without pretending procedure generated it?

### Dead World / Cave

[The Dead World And The Cave](/lens/the-dead-world-and-the-cave/) owns material-only ontology, attention capture, cave-wall reality, imagination as the captive's power, death fear, and Great Books as escape practice. Creativity-as-reception must not become a general metaphysical page about consciousness, God, Monad, or spiritual reality. It can touch the cave page only where a dead material/procedural account blocks access to imagination or received reality.

That distinction matters because the Secret History #9 source contains both the creativity passage and later cosmic machinery. A public creativity mutation could easily overclaim by making Jiang's autobiographical and metaphysical language into site-level epistemology. The held seed avoids that by treating the material as Jiang's model and by requiring craft, evidence, argument, or return as a test.

### Free Will / Asha

[Free Will As Cosmic Burden](/lens/free-will-as-cosmic-burden/) owns Asha as truth-becoming, self-destruction of false knowledge, responsibility, and chosen agency. Creativity-as-reception should not absorb Asha. The Asha source supports the proposal only as a moral test: reception is not passive inspiration or private fantasy. The received thing has to be burned through, negated, rebuilt, and returned to others.

If a future mark says fallibility, pain, disobedience, or individuality makes creativity possible, route first by the active mechanism. If the source is about choice under cosmic burden, use free will. If it is about a received whole made shareable through later craft, the creativity seed may apply.

### Human Heart And Living School

[Human Heart As Civilizational Measure](/lens/human-heart-as-civilizational-measure/) owns creation as one need inside the broader heart structure: structure, meaning, purpose, love, creation, learning, growth, curiosity, agency, compassion, guilt, forgiveness, and relation. [Living School For Psychohistory](/lens/living-school-for-psychohistory/) owns the positive institutional dream where Great Books, history, prediction, model correction, human-heart variables, democracy, and student formation prepare people to make futures.

Creativity-as-reception should not become a softer "humans need creativity" page or a second living-school page. It is narrower than both: origin before process, then backward craft and public test.

### Bureaucracy

[Bureaucracy As Institutional Death](/lens/bureaucracy-as-institutional-death/) owns institutions that preserve procedure while draining openness, feedback, imagination, risk, and founding genius. Creativity-as-reception may explain why mature schools, universities, and scientific systems miss the people who receive dangerous ideas, but bureaucracy owns the institutional machine that filters them out.

This boundary keeps the proposal from becoming generic anti-bureaucracy. The useful diagnostic is not "bureaucracy blocks creativity" by itself. It is whether a procedure claims to generate thought while the source says living thought arrived first and procedure came afterward.

## Promotion Boundary

Do not promote yet.

Promotion to an atlas relation becomes justified if future public pages repeatedly need the same bridge: received whole, backward craft, procedure as downstream, and moral/public validation. Promotion to a full page should require more than examples of creativity. It should teach a diagnostic sequence that neighboring pages cannot teach cleanly.

Until then, route by active mechanism:

- school formation, trust, obedience, research habits, and reward structure go to Education;
- language, memory, poetic world-making, and subconscious entry go to Poetry;
- living scripture, commandless coordination, and narrative reality go to Stories;
- material-only ontology, cave-wall capture, and Great Books escape practice go to Dead World / Cave;
- Asha, agency, self-negation, and responsibility go to Free Will;
- creation as human need, relation, love, guilt, and forgiveness go to Human Heart;
- psychohistory formation and democratic future-making go to Living School;
- institutional filtering of genius, credential gatekeeping, and procedure-preservation go to Bureaucracy.

Use **Creativity As Reception** only when the received-whole/backward-craft mechanism is explicit enough that routing by topic alone would lose Jiang's pressure.
