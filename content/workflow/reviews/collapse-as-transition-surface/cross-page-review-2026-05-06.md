## Review Question

Should **Collapse As Transition Surface** move from reviewed proposal seed into a public lens page now, or should it remain a boundary proposal until neighboring pages need a public collapse-transition page?

The proposal is source-backed and useful, but its danger is breadth. It touches Bronze Age collapse, Greek creativity, empire decline, financial collapse, controlled-collapse game theory, mobile capital, and resilience after efficiency. Those are not false connections. The review question is whether a public page would clarify the atlas, or merely gather material already owned by stronger neighboring concepts.

## Promotion Readiness

Outcome: **hold as a reviewed proposal seed**.

The review supports the proposal's boundary but does not support immediate public promotion. A public page would clarify the atlas only if it stays narrower than the neighboring pages:

- Collapse-transition owns the crossing from one order to another.
- Civilization owns the inner arrangement that becomes brittle or newly formed.
- Borderland owns pressure converted into energy, openness, and cohesion.
- Strategy-material-test owns war plans, escalation, logistics, and material audit.
- Mass society owns scale as a political and spiritual constraint.
- Living school owns the constructive formation response.
- Secret society owns hidden coordination and portable trust.
- Power-as-alchemy owns abstraction becoming lived force.
- Attention-capital owns extraction of attention, anxiety, mobility, and no-homeland capital.

## Promotion Conditions

Promote only after one of these becomes true:

1. A later source gives collapse-transition as an explicit reusable mechanism rather than a set of adjacent cases.
2. A public page is needed to prevent several existing concept pages from growing duplicate collapse sections.
3. The page can add durable lens points that episodes can cite without becoming generic decline commentary.
4. The engineered-collapse material can be written with clear attribution, caveats, and separation from verified external fact claims.
5. The diagnostics can be made reader-useful outside one lecture: what hidden decline has accumulated, who benefits from timing, what assets or authority move, what capacities survive, and what new human or institutional form appears.

## Review Outcome

Approved as a boundary-preserving review. Do not promote the proposal now. Use this review to discipline neighboring page edits: collapse material should be routed by mechanism, not by keyword. The strongest future public page would be short, diagnostic, and explicitly transitional rather than encyclopedic.
