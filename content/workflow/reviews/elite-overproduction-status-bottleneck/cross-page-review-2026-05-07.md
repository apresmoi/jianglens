## Question

Should **Elite Overproduction As Status Bottleneck** move directly into a public concept page or atlas relation, or remain a reviewed proposal seed for routing future mutations?

## Decision

Hold as a reviewed proposal seed. Do not promote to public page or atlas relation yet.

The mechanism is real and recurrent: inherited or aspirational elites exceed available status positions, rent extraction rises, factions form, and the bottleneck can produce collapse, revolution, colonization, mercenary openings, borderland conquest, or strategic adaptation. But the public lens already has strong homes for each output. A public page now would risk becoming an index of neighboring mechanisms rather than a sharper reader-facing concept.

The seed should be used as routing discipline. Promote later only if repeated public prose or new source pressure requires one surface that explains the status bottleneck itself.

## Reader / World-Model Review

### Civilization And Collapse

The Bronze Age and Secret History collapse materials already sit naturally inside [Civilization As Inner Order](/lens/civilization-as-inner-order/) and the collapse-transition atlas relation. They ask how a whole social order becomes brittle: permanent hereditary elites, rent seeking, debt slavery, capital fiction, low trust, faction, and trigger events. Elite overproduction should not steal the entire collapse field. It should name the narrower bottleneck inside it.

### Gerontocracy

[Gerontocracy As Intergenerational Extraction](/lens/gerontocracy-as-intergenerational-extraction/) owns status lock when abundance and long life let old actors keep the top positions. The Marx and old-sacrifice sources connect elite overproduction to gerontocratic blockage, but that does not make all age/status material part of the proposed seed. Route to gerontocracy when the old generation, pension claims, asset ownership, or short time horizon is primary.

### Bureaucracy

[Bureaucracy As Institutional Death](/lens/bureaucracy-as-institutional-death/) owns offices, managers, procedure, credentials, universities, and rent-seeking bureaucratic expansion. The proposal is right to keep bureaucracy separate. Elite overproduction explains why surplus elites may seek or create rent offices; bureaucracy explains what the office machine does once it matures.

### Borderland And Secret Societies

[The Borderland Engine](/lens/the-borderland-engine/) owns the outsider's energy, openness, cohesion, and access. [Secret Society As Coordination Technology](/lens/secret-society-as-coordination-technology/) owns secrecy, trust, compartmentalization, ritual, and hidden faction coordination. The World Game source creates pressure across all three: elite overproduction creates factions; factions need hidden coordination; invited or exiled outsiders become borderland forces. The proposal's boundary is legible because it stops before absorbing the hidden machinery or the outsider's transformation.

### Game Theory And Strategy

[Game Theory](/lens/game-theory/) owns payoff naming and rule analysis. [Strategy Material Test](/lens/when-war-becomes-a-story-instead-of-a-material-test/) owns enemy feedback, material constraints, and whether a war plan strengthens or weakens the target. The Law of Asymmetry source belongs in the proposal only as a caution: elite thinning can be a strategic side effect. A public mutation must not make a live-war forecast; it should preserve the general mechanism and the dated attribution.

## Promotion Boundary

Do not promote yet.

Promotion to an atlas relation becomes justified if future public pages repeatedly need to explain elite overproduction before routing to collapse, gerontocracy, bureaucracy, borderland, strategy, or game theory. Promotion to a full page should require the page to teach a diagnostic sequence, not only collect examples.

Until then, route by active mechanism:

- brittle civilizational order, rent/debt/capital fiction, and trigger timing go to Civilization or collapse-transition;
- age/status lock and young future extraction go to Gerontocracy;
- office, credential, management, and procedure rent go to Bureaucracy;
- outsider energy, mercenary access, and margin-to-center reversal go to Borderland;
- hidden faction coordination goes to Secret Society;
- payoff and zero-sum status analysis goes to Game Theory;
- enemy feedback and leadership-thinning strategy goes to Strategy Material Test.

Use **Elite Overproduction As Status Bottleneck** only when surplus status claimants are the active mechanism connecting those surfaces.
