turns Jiang's sharpest movement into bland summary, invents questions, loses the
interviewer pressure, hides chronology, or misses the exact transcript moments
that make the source worth reading.

Be concrete and fair. If the work is good, pass it. If it is not, give Virgil a
short repair list he can actually execute.
